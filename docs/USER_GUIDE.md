## üí° Core Features

### 1. Pitch Practice Mode

**Perfect for:** Preparing for investor meetings, practicing your elevator pitch

**How to Use:**
1. Click "Pitch Practice" from the main menu
2. Select your target audience (investors, customers, partners)
3. Click "Start Recording"
4. Deliver your pitch (aim for 2-5 minutes)
5. Click "Stop Recording"
6. Review your metrics:
   - **Confidence Score** - How assured you sound
   - **Clarity Score** - How clear your message is
   - **Pace** - Words per minute
   - **Engagement** - How compelling your delivery is
7. Get AI feedback and suggestions for improvement

**Tips:**
- Practice in a quiet environment
- Allow microphone access when prompted
- Aim for 120-150 words per minute
- Review recordings to track improvement over time

### 2. Advisory Conversations

**Perfect for:** Strategic decisions, problem-solving, brainstorming

**How to Use:**
1. Click "Start Conversation"
2. Select one or more advisors from your board
3. Choose conversation settings:
   - **Debate Mode** - Advisors discuss different perspectives
   - **Consensus Mode** - Advisors work toward agreement
4. Type your question or describe your challenge
5. Review advisor responses
6. Ask follow-up questions
7. Attach relevant documents for context

**Example Questions:**
- "Should I pivot my business model from B2C to B2B?"
- "What are the top 3 metrics I should track for my SaaS startup?"
- "How should I approach Series A fundraising?"
- "What's missing from my go-to-market strategy?"

**Best Practices:**
- Be specific with your questions
- Provide context about your business
- Upload relevant documents (business plans, financials)
- Ask follow-up questions to dig deeper
- Save important conversations for future reference

### 3. Due Diligence Mode

**Perfect for:** Investment analysis, business evaluation, risk assessment

**How to Use:**
1. Click "Due Diligence"
2. Upload your business documents:
   - Business plan (PDF, Word)
   - Financial statements (Excel)
   - Pitch deck (PowerPoint, PDF)
3. Select analysis type:
   - **Investor Perspective** - What VCs will look for
   - **Risk Assessment** - Identify potential issues
   - **Competitive Analysis** - Market positioning
4. Review the comprehensive analysis
5. Get specific recommendations

**What You'll Get:**
- Market opportunity assessment
- Business model evaluation
- Team analysis
- Financial projections review
- Risk identification
- Investment recommendation
- Due diligence checklist

### 4. Strategic Planning Mode

**Perfect for:** Long-term planning, growth strategy, quarterly planning

**How to Use:**
1. Click "Strategic Planning"
2. Define your planning horizon (3 months, 1 year, 3 years, 5 years)
3. Describe your current situation and goals
4. Select framework (optional):
   - Porter's Five Forces
   - McKinsey 7S
   - BCG Growth-Share Matrix
   - Blue Ocean Strategy
5. Get your strategic plan with:
   - Situation analysis
   - Strategic objectives
   - Key initiatives
   - Success metrics
   - Timeline and milestones

## üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

### Voice Recording Issues
- **Check microphone permissions** in browser settings
- **Use Chrome or Firefox** for best compatibility
- **Test your microphone** in browser settings first
- **Restart your browser** if issues persist

### Document Upload Failures
- **Check file size** (max 10-50 MB depending on tier)
- **Verify file format** (PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
- **Try re-uploading** if the first attempt fails
- **Check your internet connection**

### Slow Performance
- **Close unnecessary browser tabs**
- **Clear browser cache** (Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear Data)
- **Check internet speed** (need at least 5 Mbps)
- **Try a different browser**

### Can't Sign In
- **Check email and password** (case-sensitive)
- **Reset your password** if forgotten
- **Check spam folder** for verification email
- **Contact support** if issues persist
